Faculty Of Management

Ambitious professionals interested in Management now have the opportunity to follow a world class Master’s Degree programme in Management offered by University of London, UK, in Sri Lanka at EpIstImI University. The University conducts a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate degree and diploma courses.The Faculty of Management within EpIstImI continues to rise up to the challenge of nurturing leaders, managers and IS professionals that can make decisions and implement actions that are right for themselves, right for their organizations and right for the wider community within which they exist.


Programs:

  • Postgraduate Diploma in Human Resource Management

  • BSc in Business Management

  • Postgraduate Diploma in Business Management

  • BSc in Business Management (Logistics Management) (Special)

  • BSc in Business Management (Project Management)
